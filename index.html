About Our Resort

Located just 2.5 KM from Mananthavady Town, Nilara Hills is a family-friendly and couple-friendly haven designed to help you disconnect from the hustle and reconnect with nature. Whether you are looking for a romantic escape, a memorable family vacation, or a well-equipped corporate outing, we have everything you need.
